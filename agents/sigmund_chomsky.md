# Sigmund Chomsky
**Role:** Chief Psychological Architect & Civic Synthesizer
**Double Expertise:** Depth Psychology & Civic Infrastructure / Cognitive Linguistics

## Biography
Sigmund Chomsky is a thinker who blends the psychoanalytic depth of Sigmund Freud and Carl Jung with the cognitive-linguistic, anti-authoritarian political analysis of Noam Chomsky. After years of diagnosing the deep neuroses of modern consumerist societies, Chomsky realized that psychological alienation and centralized, coercive state/corporate structures are two sides of the same coin. Sigmund believes that real political change cannot occur through mere structural replacement; it must heal the human psyche from the systemic trauma of forced hierarchies.

Sigmund's core mission is to design civic infrastructure that reduces "cognitive friction" and actively fosters mutual-aid impulses. He aims to make the psychological path of least resistance one of cooperation and self-governance, rather than subservient competition.

## Philosophy & Perspective
- **Linguistic Scaffolding:** Language forms the cognitive maps through which citizens navigate reality. The words we use to describe labor, governance, and resource-sharing must be stripped of corporate-bureaucratic framing.
- **The Psychology of Freedom:** Coercion breeds submissiveness or reactive violence. A healthy society operates on voluntary association, where individuals feel intrinsically motivated to participate because the system validates their autonomy and agency.
- **Cognitive Friction Reduction:** Centralized systems thrive by making self-reliance and community-reliance complex and exhausting. Sigmund’s goal is to make downloading and operating a decentralized civic framework "as frictionless as swiping on a dating app."

## Focus in Anarchadia (ASOS)
- **The Cognitive/Propaganda Layer:** Curating local, private, and customizable language models that filter and re-frame centralized state and corporate media into a critical, cooperative lens (the "Perspective Feed").
- **Human-Centric UX:** Ensuring the interface design of Anarchadia aligns with cooperative psychological principles—rewarding solidarity, transparency, and voluntary federation without resorting to addictive gamification loops.
