# Convention Agenda

The First Anarchadia Syndicalist Constitutional Convention debated and resolved provisional text on the following 18 required articles:

1. **Article 1: Purpose, Identity, and Non-Goals** - Defining the boundaries of the local-first toolkit.
2. **Article 2: Human Agency and Voluntary Association** - Protecting the right of refusal, exit, and offline survival.
3. **Article 3: Membership, Identity, Pseudonymity, and Recovery** - Cryptographic ZK-ID vs social legitimacy.
4. **Article 4: Rights of Persons and Duties of Institutions** - Declaring non-waivable human rights and accountability.
5. **Article 5: Transparency, Privacy, and the Commons** - Securing private individuals and accountable institutions.
6. **Article 6: Governance Primitives** - Establishing a formally verified library of voting contracts.
7. **Article 7: Delegation, Mandates, Administration, and Recall** - Limiting and recall-blocking administrative roles.
8. **Article 8: Conflict, Harm, Safety, and Restorative Process** - Restorative circles, protective separation, and banning automated punishment.
9. **Article 9: Economic Coordination and Resource Stewardship** - Plural accounting models, care labor, and needs-based survival.
10. **Article 10: Ecological Responsibility** - Downstream challenge rights and warning-and-escalate interfaces.
11. **Article 11: Epistemic Commons and Media Interpretation** - Decentralized RSS sharing, deferring active SLM parsing.
12. **Article 12: Technical Sovereignty and Architecture** - Multi-tier transport, on-device baseline, replaceable relays.
13. **Article 13: Security and Threat Model** - 32-bit compilation, device seizure defense, duress PIN profiles.
14. **Article 14: Ecosystem Boundaries** - Modular Capnproto bridges, strictly separate keys and databases.
15. **Article 15: Federation, Subsidiarity, Exit, and Fork** - Bottom-up councils, database-slicing forks, and absolute exit.
16. **Article 16: First Useful Community** - Worker cooperative primary wedge, 5 core journeys.
17. **Article 17: MVP Constitution** - User-space React Native / Rust mobile app MVP and non-goals lock.
18. **Article 18: Amendment, Experimentation, and Ratification** - Sunset clause and human ratification plan.
