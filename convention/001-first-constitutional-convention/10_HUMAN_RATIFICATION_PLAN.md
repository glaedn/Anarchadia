# Human Ratification and Sunset Plan

## 1. Provisional Status and Sunset Clause
This entire constitution is provisional and is strictly a set of design recommendations from the synthetic council of experts. Every article will **sunset automatically within 12 months** from deployment unless reviewed, amended, and ratified by real-world physical human assemblies.

## 2. Target Human Assemblies
Ratification efforts will focus strictly on direct-democratic neighborhood assemblies, housing cooperatives, and physical worker cooperatives (such as cooperative bakeries, printing shops, and agricultural communes).

## 3. Method of Ratification
1. **Educational Outreach:** Provide non-technical, printed, and localized explainers of every provisional article.
2. **Assembly Deliberation:** Allocate a mandatory 14-day reflection period for assemblies to read, debate, and suggest amendments.
3. **ZK Ballot Session:** Conduct physical or mesh-based ratification voting using Zero-Knowledge pseudonymous credentials, protecting participants from state-level or local retaliation.
4. **Veto and Custom Patches:** Human assemblies retain the sovereign right to reject any specific provisional article, writing and compiling their own customized local constitutional patches.

## 4. Ratification Criteria
- An article is declared ratified by a community when it receives simple majority (or configured consensus) in the community's local assembly.
- Sibling systems must remain separable, so that a community may choose to ratify Cerbanimo work tasks without ratifying Anarchadia's default voting primitives.
