# Convention Charter: ASCCON

## 1. Purpose
The purpose of the First Anarchadia Syndicalist Constitutional Convention (ASCCON) is to transition Anarchadia from a speculative, visionary blueprint (Vision Pass 0) into an operationally hardened, agency-centered product and architectural specification (Implementation Pass 1).

## 2. Authority and Limits
The authority of this convention is strictly provisional. Synthetic agent votes and consensus represent design recommendations, technical stress-tests, and architectural constraints. They hold no democratic legitimacy and cannot waive, override, or substitute for the consent, self-determination, or ratification of real human beings and affected communities.

## 3. Shared Epistemic Rules
All participating delegates must:
- Distinguish Fact, Assumption, Prediction, Value Judgment, and Metaphor.
- Cite historical precedents, field research, or technical specs for claims.
- Reject claims of absolute privacy, perfect decentralization, objective interpretation, or mathematically proven justice.
- Openly state uncertainty levels and preserve disagreements.
- Identify affected people who are absent.
- Prefer reversible, small-scale experiments over permanent technical and constitutional doctrines.

## 4. Voting Rules
- Each delegate receives exactly one vote.
- Ordinary provisions require simple majority (4/6).
- Rights-critical provisions require 5 of 6 votes and no unresolved primary-jurisdiction rights objection.
- A tie fails.
- Synthetic votes never equal community ratification.

## 5. Debate Rules
- Opening motion drafted by Merlin Sol.
- Six preliminary testimonies.
- Cross-examination challenges.
- Explicit position changes.
- Alternatives and amendments.
- Final votes.
- Product/architecture consequences.
